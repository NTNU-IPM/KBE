Knowledge Based Engineering (KBE) is a technology that is typically used for introducing automation in engineering work. This technology has been used with great success in Aker Solutions over the last 10-15 years. The KBE applications are based on an object-oriented programming language called AML, created by Technosoft Inc. in Ohio, USA.
